What makes a great lifestyle image?

A lifestyle image shows your product being used naturally by a real person in a real setting. It should help the shopper picture themselves using it. Think: a skincare product being applied in a bright bathroom, a pet toy being played with by a happy dog, or a kitchen gadget in action on a clean countertop. It should feel authentic, aspirational, and relatable — not staged or obviously fake.
